# CONCEPT C: SPATIAL LAYERS

## AI Corp Executive Interface — Immersive Depth Design

---

## Visual Direction

### Philosophy: "The Intelligent Canvas"

Imagine stepping into a command room where information exists in space, not on a screen. Spatial Layers transforms the AI Corp interface into a living dimensional canvas where hierarchy is expressed through literal depth, relationships through proximity, and importance through elevation.

Inspired by visionOS spatial computing principles adapted for 2D screens, this concept creates the *illusion* of a three-dimensional workspace using sophisticated layering, parallax scrolling, and physics-based animations. Each UI element floats at its own altitude in a carefully choreographed spatial hierarchy.
