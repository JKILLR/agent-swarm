## 1. LIGHT MODE VARIANT: "Daylight Terminal"

### Philosophy

A terminal doesn't have to be dark. The Daylight variant maintains all the precision and information density of Command Terminal while being gentler on eyes in bright environments and meeting accessibility requirements for users who struggle with dark interfaces.
