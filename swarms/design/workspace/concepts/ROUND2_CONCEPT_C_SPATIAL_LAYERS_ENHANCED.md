## 1. DARK MODE VARIANT: Spatial Void

The original Spatial Layers concept was built on a light foundation. This dark variant maintains the same depth philosophy while adapting for low-light environments and user preference.

### Dark Mode Philosophy

**SPATIAL VOID** inverts the paradigm: instead of floating glass panels over a light canvas, we have luminous glass surfaces emerging from deep space. The metaphor shifts from "daylight control room" to "observatory at night" — stars (agents) glow in the darkness.
