## 1. Visual Direction

### Mood & Feeling

**POWERFUL â€¢ TECHNICAL â€¢ IN-CONTROL â€¢ PRECISE**

This concept draws from the visceral experience of a master technician at their terminalâ€”the satisfying clatter of commands, the instant feedback, the raw power of direct system access. It's not "hacker aesthetic" as costume; it's functional brutalism refined for executive command.

**Core Visual Pillars:**

1. **Scanline Heritage** â€” Subtle horizontal lines evoke CRT monitors, grounding the interface in computing history while feeling distinctly modern
2. **Data Density Without Clutter** â€” Every pixel earns its place. Information-rich but never overwhelming
3. **Command Confidence** â€” UI elements respond instantly, animations are crisp and purposeful (no bounce, no wobble)
4. **Brutalist Precision** â€” Sharp corners, hard edges, geometric purity. No decoration for decoration's sake

**Mood Keywords:**
- Mission Control

- System32
- Root Access
- Kernel Space

On mobile, the terminal aesthetic adapts:
- Bottom navigation bar replaces sidebar
- Cards stack vertically
- Command palette remains (tap + hold to invoke)
- Gestures: swipe for navigation, long-press for context menu
